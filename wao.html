About Us

Hello! My name is Cruzz Eltone, and I am the founder of We Are One. In June 2024, I decided to take action after realizing how many people were suffering from mental health struggles, but lacked the support they needed. I turned to TikTok to connect with those struggling and found a community that needed to be heard. We Are One is a platform dedicated to breaking the stigma surrounding mental health and providing a safe space where people can share their stories and find support.
